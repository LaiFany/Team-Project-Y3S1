Cash Management

Hong Leong Online Business (HLOB) offers corporate customers secured online business banking and convenience via internet. This service allows customers to access real time information and managed their cash flows at their office or while they are on the go. Hong Leong Online Business (HLOB) is user friendly, available 7 days a week with secured encryption technology. We offer you Simple, Secure and Time Saving online banking.

1. (a) e-Inquiry
e-Inquiry allows you to access your company's latest account information online for:

  • Business Current Account Activity
  • Fixed Deposits
  • Loans Balances
  • Cheques Status
  • Current Account Statements
  • Forex Rates
  • Money Market Rates

1-b e-Trade Inquiry
e-Trade Inquiry allows you to access your latest trade transactions and facilities details online for:

  • Trade Facilities
  • Trade Product Outstanding
  • Trade Transactions History

2. e-Payments
In addition to the e-Inquiry facility, e-Payments allows you to perform payment transactions online for:

  • Single Payment (Intra and IBG)
  • Payroll (Intrabank and IBG)
  • Bulk Payment (for vendor and supplier)
  • RENTAS
  • Foreign TT
  • Bill Payment
  • Loan Repayment
  • eShare
  • Statutory Payment (EPF, SOCSO and IRB)
  • Lembaga Tabung Haji (LTH)
  • eDividend
  • Cheque Book Request
  • Stop Cheque Requests
  • e-Procurement

3. e-Collection
Hong Leong Bank will collect your regular receivables based on your instruction by debiting the buyers' accounts maintained with Hong Leong Bank or other banks. Collection transaction details will be provided to ease your company's account reconciliations.

  • Direct Debit
  • MEPS Direct Debit

4. Liquidity Management
Auto sweep facility provides you with a convenient way in managing your daily account balances.

  • Auto Sweep
