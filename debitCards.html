Hong Leong Debit Card

The Hong Leong Debit Card is an ATM and payment card that is linked to your Hong Leong Current or Savings Account. You can withdraw cash via the ATM machines like the conventional ATM card and also make purchases worldwide!

1. No RM50 Government Service Tax (GST)

  • Debit Card will not be charged RM50 GST

2. ATM Cash Withdrawal

  • Withdraw cash locally and overseas from over 1 million ATM machines that display the logo MEPS, VISA or PLUS

3. Payment Convenience

  • Make purchases at over 29 million VISA, MEPS and BANKCARD merchants worldwide You can also pay bills, transfer money and make purchases online! 

*Based on standard industry practice, for any amount of petrol transactions, RM200 will be placed on hold for 3 days in your Hong Leong Savings or Current Account. The account balance will be reduced by the amount on hold and Cardholders will not be able to access this fund until the petrol transaction has been presented by the petrol companies to the bank to make payment. After which, the amount will be released after deducting the actual petrol transaction.

4. Security

  • In the event your card is lost or stolen, and if any unauthorised transactions have been made on your card, your losses will be limited to RM150*. Our Contact Centre is open 24/7 to assist you in blocking your Debit Card in order to prevent any misuse in case of it being lost or stolen. 
* Provided you have exercise diligent care in safeguarding the card and promptly reported its loss to the Bank (For card loss in Malaysia) or the VISA Travel Service Centre or its nearest affiliates (For card loss overseas). Notification has to be made to the Bank via telephone, telegram, facsimile or e-mail and confirmation of the same has to be made in writing, accompanied by a police report.

5. Flexibility of Account Selection

  • Choose to link your Debit Card to any Current or Savings Account of your choice and the account will be automatically debited when you make a purchase

6. Better Financial Management

  • View your itemized monthly Debit Card statements online for FREE. Alternatively, you can request for hardcopy monthly statements to be sent to the address recorded in Hong Leong Bank's record at a nominal fee of RM2 per month Set your "Daily Retail Purchase Limit", "Withdrawal Limit" and "Online Purchase Limit" based on your comfort level (Capped at a maximum of RM5,000) via Hong Leong Connect, phone banking or visit any of our branches nationwide Purchases made with your Debit Card are withdrawn from your Hong Leong Savings or Current Account, hence compel you to spend within your means

Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card

The Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card is a Touch'n Go Card with an automatic reload feature that is linked to your Hong Leong Savings or Current Account via Hong Leong Debit Card. Every time your Zing Debit Card balance falls below RM50, it will automatically reload RM50 and the amount will be debited from your Hong Leong Savings or Current Account via Hong Leong Debit Card.

1. No RM50 Government Service Tax (GST)

  • Zing Debit Card will not be charged RM50 GST

2. Automatic Reload Function

  • The Card will auto-reload RM50 when balance falls below RM50 and the amount will be debited from Hong Leong Savings or Current Account via Hong Leong Debit Card or define as Retail Purchase Account.

3. Pre-loaded of RM50

  • Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card is pre-loaded with RM50 and will be debited from Retail Purchase Account when you apply

4. Linked to Hong Leong Debit Card

  • Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card is linked to Hong Leong Debit Card and this offer is only applicable to Hong Leong Debit Cardholders. A sum of RM100 will be earmarked from the Retail Purchase Account.

5. Free E-Statement

  • Free e-Statement via www.touchngo.com.my to view the auto reload transaction details.

6. Beyond the Road

  • Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card is accepted use on public transport, carparks, petrol, retail chains restaurants, healthcare and many mores.
  • For 18 years old and above
  • Apply for a Hong Leong Debit Card and automatically qualify to apply for a Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card
  • If you are an existing Hong Leong Debit Cardholder, just walk into any Hong Leong Bank branch nationwide with your Hong Leong Debit Card and apply for the NEW Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card
  • One (1) Debit Cardholder eligible for one (1) Hong Leong Touch'n Go Zing Debit Card.
