Making online payment anytime, anywhere with FPX!
Financial Process Exchange (FPX) is an online payment gateway that allows you to make real time online payments using FPX participating banks' current or savings account.

It's simple and convenience!
You can now make online payment anytime at anywhere with FPX. FPX service is available from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm daily, seven days a week, 365 days a year. However, FPX service is also dependent on the Internet Banking's service availability at the participating banks.

All you need to have : -

  • own a current or savings account with the FPX participating banks
  • registered for Internet Banking service with the FPX participating banks
  • get ready the payment information such as bill number, seller reference number and related information to enable merchants to identify the payment
Step 1 : Login to participating merchants' web page and enter payment details.
Step 2 : Select Hong Leong Bank as debiting bank via FPX
Step 3 : You will be directed to Hong Leong Connect website and key in your User name and Password
Step 4 : Upon approval, transaction value will be debited from your Hong Leong Bank account
Step 5 : You will receive an email notification confirming on the transaction
